Our Showcase

Our computer labs are equipped with the latest PCs and workstations, and each machine is equipped with high-speed processors, high-capacity memory, and high-performance graphics. These devices can support a wide range of tasks, from basic programming to complex graphic design and video editing.

We provide students with a wide range of software resources, including but not limited to operating systems, office software, programming language environments, database management systems, and professional software such as AutoCAD, Adobe Creative Suite, etc. The software covers all levels, from basic to professional, and caters to the needs of different disciplines and interests.

The school has a high-speed and stable internet connection, ensuring that students do not face any barriers when it comes to online learning, research, and collaboration. Our cyber security system also ensures the security and privacy of data.

The university's computer science program is known for its outstanding educational achievements, and its students have won many awards in national and international competitions, published many academic papers, and applied for numerous patents. We pay attention to practical teaching, build a full-chain practical training system, and cultivate a large number of high-quality computer professionals, and our graduates are widely praised in the industry.
