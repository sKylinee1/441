The Software Development major aims to equip students with the entire process of software development, from requirement analysis to design, coding, testing, and maintenance. Students will learn various programming languages, including but not limited to Java, C#, and JavaScript, as well as best practices and agile development methodologies in software development.

The Cybersecurity major focuses on protecting the security of information systems, including networks, software, and data. Students will learn how to identify and defend against cyber threats, including hacking attacks, malware, and data breaches. The curriculum covers encryption techniques, cybersecurity protocols, penetration testing, and security policy formulation.

The Artificial Intelligence and Data Science major is dedicated to cultivating students' expertise in the fields of artificial intelligence and data analysis. Students will learn machine learning algorithms, deep learning, data mining, and big data processing technologies, as well as how to apply these technologies to solve practical problems.
